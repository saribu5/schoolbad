the purpose of this website is for him and his classmates to be able to play unblocked games while school is in session.

you may ask this: "why use this website when there are hundreds of other unblocked games websites out there?" and my answer to that question is pretty simple. you really don't need to use this website.

i created this website for mostly my own use. the games on this website are mostly games that i tend to play pretty often.
